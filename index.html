John Romero

John Romero was born on October 28, 1967, son of Alfonso Antonio Romero and Ginny Schuneman. John started programming on an Apple II in 1980. The first game despite never being published, he made was a Crazy Climbers clone while his first published game was Scout Search. In July 2001 Romero cofounded Monkeystone Games in order to develop mobile games. Monkeystone Games lasted 3 and a half years and made around 15 games. In March 2010 Romero was a guest editor of Retro Gamer magazine. In 2004 Romero married Raluca Alexandra Ple»ôca, and they established Romero Games in augest 2015.
